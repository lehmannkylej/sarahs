Custom Cakes

Whether the intention of your celebration is simple elegance, vintage charm, dramatic glitter, or whimsical, a Sarah’s Custom Cake is the perfect choice for special celebrations or those where custom design, flavor, or color matching is required. Our Cake Designers build your dream cake from the ground up, taking your vision and creating something that is unique to your event.

ORDERING A CUSTOM CAKE

To order a custom cake, please email us or book online to request an appointment with one of our cake designers. Be sure to indicate the date of your event so we can check for availability. Any order including a custom cake will start at $150 and include a 45-minute consultation with our Custom Cake Consultant.

Custom Cake Flavors

Our custom cakes are 3-layers of cake, 2 layers of filling, and 5” tall. Custom cakes include a Classic Cake Flavor with your choice of our tradiontal buttercream or vanilla buttercream for your filling and icing. Other flavors and fillings are an additonal charge.

ABOUT US

Sarah’s is a locally-owned business that was established in 2003. What began as a small café & catering company in Wildwood, Missouri, turned into a specialized dessert destination.

In 2010, Sarah’s made the move to Chesterfield, Missouri, and focused more on custom cakes, desserts, and cupcakes. At that time, we also added St. Louis' first dessert truck to our family. 2015 brought on more expansion and the need for a second dessert truck.

While the sweeter side of the business continued to grow, we never lost our passion for our small café roots, so in 2016 we decided to bring our extensive dessert and updated café menus to historic Old Towne Eureka, Missouri.
